p-2011-102 – Golf science

Set by Dr Petar Igic ( p.igic@swansea.ac.uk ), Multidisciplinary Nanotechnology Centre ( MNC ).

Description

The students will investigate physics equations in order to develop an analytical model describing how far golf balls can be propelled based on the force, impulse and angle of projection. He/she will also explore the effects that the various lengths of golf clubs produce and how they affect the distance the ball can be hit.

Cross disciplinary research theme(s): Health and Sport
